Wind Sensor System

This automated system tells you whether or not to wear a jacket when you go outside, depending on whether or not the wind speed is over a certain speed.

While the LED is green, you will be fine without a jacket. If the LED turns red, you should put on a jacket.

The product comes in two parts that work together.
